Embedded ISP Program (EIP)

Redefining Internet Capacity Economics

The Fastly Embedded ISP Program empowers Internet Service Providers to scale smarter by embedding Fastly’s multi-tenant Points of Presence within ISP networks. This program combines infrastructure deployment and business collaboration to unlock performance and shared revenue—without the cost of traditional infrastructure buildouts.

Infrastructure Collaboration

Business Alignment

Fastly’s model includes revenue sharing and aligned incentives for long-term success.
