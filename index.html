Embedded ISP Program (EIP)

Partnering to redefine the capacity building economics for peak demand

A Partnership on Two Levels

Infrastructure Collaboration

Fastly embeds multi-tenant PoPs directly into ISP networks to deliver local capacity where it's most needed. These PoPs are fully integrated into Fastly's global platform, supporting 4K, AR, VR, and low-latency use cases.

Operational Alignment

We work hand-in-hand with ISPs and content providers to ensure joint capacity planning, aligned traffic delivery, and end-to-end visibility for improved performance and cost efficiency.

Why Join EIP?

  • For End Users: Enjoy seamless, high-quality digital experiences with reduced latency and stable live streaming.
  • For Content Providers: Gain reliable access to additional peak capacity and maintain a high quality of experience across networks.
  • For ISPs: Reduce infrastructure CapEx, improve network utilization, and deliver superior user performance.
  • For Fastly: Expand global reach with local efficiency, ensuring delivery where demand is highest.

Who Is This For?

The Embedded ISP Program is designed for:

  • ISPs seeking to cost-effectively scale network capacity to meet peak traffic demands.
  • Operators aiming to improve end-user quality of experience without heavy investment.
  • Networks that want to monetize edge delivery opportunities through stronger alignment with global CDNs and content flows.

Federated into Fastly’s Global Network

Embedded PoPs are seamlessly integrated into Fastly's platform, presented to content providers as standard PoPs, and managed with the same performance features as any regional location. Each embedded site strengthens Fastly's global coverage and increases capacity availability per ASN.
