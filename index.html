Embedded ISP Program (EIP)

A new kind of partnership built for performance and shared value

Redefining the Economics of Internet Capacity

The Fastly Embedded ISP Program empowers Internet Service Providers to scale smarter by embedding Fastly’s multi-tenant Points of Presence (PoPs) within ISP networks. This infrastructure and business partnership unlocks performance and revenue opportunities without the cost of traditional buildouts.

A Partnership on Two Levels

Infrastructure Collaboration

We deploy our PoPs inside your network to support the most demanding content delivery needs.

Business Alignment

Our model includes revenue sharing and aligned incentives for long-term success.
