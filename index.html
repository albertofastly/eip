Embedded ISP Program (EIP)

Partnering to redefine the capacity building economics for peak demand

A Partnership on Two Levels

Infrastructure Collaboration

Fastly embeds multi-tenant PoPs directly into ISP networks to deliver local capacity at scale. These PoPs are fully federated into our global platform and operate just like any Fastly regional PoP.

Business Alignment

We align with ISPs and content providers on joint goals for performance, scalability, and efficiency, ensuring long-term value for all stakeholders in the ecosystem.

Why Join EIP?

  • Better for End Users: Faster, more stable access to high-quality content, including 4K video, AR, VR, and real-time applications.
  • Better for Content Providers: Predictable peak traffic delivery, higher QoE, and one global delivery platform with local presence in ISP networks.
  • Better for ISPs: Offload peak capacity, reduce CapEx, and differentiate through improved end-user QoE.
  • Better for Fastly: Aligns platform scale with infrastructure efficiency and accelerates deployment velocity.

Who Is This For?

The Embedded ISP Program is ideal for Internet Service Providers looking to:

  • Improve peak traffic delivery and end-user performance.
  • Reduce investment in new capacity while scaling faster.
  • Enhance QoE for content providers and users.
  • Be part of a global edge network with local reach and seamless integration.

Federated into Fastly’s Global Network

Fastly Embedded PoPs are seamlessly integrated with our regional infrastructure. They provide local capacity per ASN and are fully presented as standard Fastly PoPs to content providers. This approach helps expand coverage without complexity while preserving all network features and efficiencies.
