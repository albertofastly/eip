Embedded ISP Program (EIP)

A new kind of partnership built for performance and shared value

Redefining Internet Capacity Economics

The Fastly Embedded ISP Program empowers Access Internet Service Providers to scale smarter by embedding Fastly’s multi-tenant Points of Presence within ISP networks. This program combines infrastructure deployment and business collaboration to unlock performance and shared revenue without the cost of traditional infrastructure buildouts.

A Partnership on Two Levels

Infrastructure Collaboration

Fastly deploys multi-tenant Embedded PoPs inside Access ISPs’ networks to support the most demanding content delivery needs.

Business Alignment

Fastly partners with ISPs to create business models that drive efficiency, unlock growth, and elevate the quality of experience for their customers.

Why Join EIP?

  • Built for Peak Demand: We deploy where traffic is proven by top media, gaming, and entertainment platforms.
  • Technology That Scales: Backed by Perfect Request Collapsing, Direct Server Return (DSR) and AI Tiered Caching.
  • Global Reach: Embedded PoPs extend our coverage with no blind spots.
  • Aligned for the Future: Infrastructure plus revenue models scale with demand.

Who Is This For?

The Embedded ISP Program is ideal for Access Internet Service Providers with last-mile networks looking to:

  • Improve peak traffic delivery and end-user performance.
  • Reduce investment in new capacity while scaling faster.
  • Enhance QoE for content providers and users.
  • Monetize network through new revenue streams.
  • Be part of a global edge network with local reach and seamless integration.

Federated into Fastly’s Global Network

Fastly Embedded PoPs are seamlessly integrated with our regional and global infrastructure. They provide local capacity per ASN and are fully presented as standard Fastly PoPs to content providers. This approach helps expand coverage without complexity while preserving all platform features and efficiencies.
